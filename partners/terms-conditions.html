Calculation Method: Commission = Percentage × (Client's GST/ITR filing fee paid, net of discounts and taxes). Tracked in real-time via dashboard. Lifetime means ongoing as long as the client remains active and pays.

Exclusions: No commission on refunds, chargebacks, or non-GST/ITR services.

Commissions are subject to TDS under sections 194J/194-I.

2. Scope – What Data is Collected

This Privacy Policy covers the personal data we collect from you as a partner in the Partner Program. We collect various types of data to enable program participation, verify identities, process commissions, and ensure security and compliance.

The data we collect includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Identification Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth (for age verification).
  • Financial and Tax-Related Information: Permanent Account Number (PAN), Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN) if applicable, bank account details (including account number, IFSC code, and bank name for commission payouts).
  • Professional Credentials: For CA and CS partners, Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) or Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) membership number and year of registration.
  • Technical and Device Information: IP address, device type, browser user agent, operating system, and other device identifiers.
  • Referral and Program Data: Unique Partner Code, details of referred clients (such as referral timestamps, client usage of your code), commission earnings data, and payout history.
  • Other Data: Any additional information you voluntarily provide during signup, dashboard interactions, or communications with us.

3. How Data is Collected

We collect your data through secure and transparent methods to ensure accuracy and consent. The primary channels include Signup Forms, Dashboard Interactions, Payment Gateway Integrations, Automated Collection (IP logs), and Third-Party Verifications (ICAI/ICSI portals).

4. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your data solely for legitimate purposes related to the Partner Program: Verification and Onboarding, Commission Calculation and Payout (10% standard or 20% for CA/CS), Fraud Prevention, Tax Compliance (TDS reporting), and Legal requirements under DPDP and RBI guidelines.

5. Legal Basis

Our data processing is grounded in Consent, Contract Performance, Legitimate Interest (for security), and Legal Obligation (TDS, GST, and IT Act compliance).

6. Granular Consents Required

Consent is a cornerstone of our data practices. During signup, you must provide explicit consent via 5-6 separate checkboxes. These cover Data Collection, Commission Processing, Third-Party Sharing, Fraud Detection, and Tax Compliance. Opt-in for marketing is strictly separate.

7. Data Sharing

We share your data only with trusted parties like Payment Gateways, Banks, Verification Portals (ICAI/ICSI), and Tax Authorities as required by law. No data is shared for marketing without your opt-in.

8. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in India-based servers for 7 years (as per Tax laws). We use AES-256 encryption and TLS 1.3 for transit security. All financial data follows RBI security guidelines.

9. Data Subject Rights

Under DPDP, you have the right to Access, Rectification, Erasure, Restriction, Portability, and Withdrawal of Consent. Contact privacy@taxeasepro.in for requests.

10. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for session management and security. No third-party tracking for advertising is used.

11. International Data Transfer

All data processing and storage occur within India. No international transfers are made.

12. Data Breach Notification Procedure

In case of a breach, we assess and contain it within 72 hours and notify affected individuals and the Data Protection Board as per DPDP rules.

13. Children's Data

The Partner Program is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect children's data.

14. Changes to Policy & How Notified

Updates are posted on our website. We notify partners via email or dashboard at least 30 days in advance for material changes.

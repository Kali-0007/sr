    Quick FAQs

    What is Income Tax?

    Direct tax on earnings at progressive rates.

    FY vs AY for ITR?

    FY = Earn year, AY = Assess year (next FY).

    Old vs New Regime?

    New: Lower rates, fewer deductions. Old: Higher rates, more exemptions.

    GST Registration Time?

    3-5 days with docs help.

    Documents for ITR?

    Form-16, PAN, Aadhaar, bank statements.

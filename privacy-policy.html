Privacy Policy

TaxEasePro

Last Updated: January 03, 2026

At TaxEasePro, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly. We operate as an online tax service provider in India through our website at https://kali-0007.github.io/sr/. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We only collect the data we need to provide our services securely and efficiently (Data Minimization principle).

1.1 Data Collected at Signup or Login

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, mobile number, and address.
  • Security Metadata: IP address, browser details, device information, and login timestamps.

1.2 Data Collected for Our Services

To deliver tax filing services, you voluntarily upload the following through your secure dashboard:

  • Sensitive Financial Data: PAN card details, Aadhaar number (voluntary), bank account statements, Form 16, income proofs, and investment details.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Tax Services: Preparation and filing of ITR, GST returns, and handling tax notices.
  • Communication: Service updates and notifications regarding your tax status.
  • Security: Monitoring login metadata to prevent unauthorized access and fraud.

3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

Our processing is lawful under Section 7 of the DPDP Act, relying on Explicit Consent obtained via checkbox and the Voluntary Provision of documents for tax services.

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Passwords are hashed using SHA-256. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only on a "need-to-know" basis.

5. SHARING YOUR DATA

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data is shared only with Government Authorities (e.g., Income Tax Department, GSTN) as legally required for filing your returns.

6. DATA RETENTION

Tax-related documents are retained for up to 8 years as per the requirements of the Income Tax Act, 1961. Other data is deleted upon account deactivation or user request, subject to legal holds.

7. CONSENT AND PROOF

We maintain secure digital records of your consent, including the Timestamp, IP Address, and "Yes" status, as legal proof under the DPDP Act.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

As a Data Principal, you have the right to Access, Correction, Erasure, and Withdrawal of Consent. All requests are processed within 15 days.

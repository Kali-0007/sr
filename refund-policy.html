Refund & Cancellation Policy

TaxEasePro

Last Updated: January 03, 2026

At TaxEasePro, we strive to provide high-quality, accurate, and timely tax compliance services. This policy explains when and how refunds are processed for our Services.

1. GENERAL REFUND RULE: Once our team has started processing your request (reviewing documents or initiating filing), the fee is non-refundable.

2. EXCEPTIONS – WHEN REFUNDS MAY BE ISSUED

We review requests on a case-by-case basis in the following scenarios:

  • Wrong Service Selected: If informed before processing begins.
  • Technical Error: Double charges or system glitches on our end.
  • Service Non-Delivery: Failure to deliver due to reasons under our control.

3. REFUND PROCESSING TIME

Approved refunds are processed within 3-7 business days. The amount will reflect in your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank.

4. NON-REFUNDABLE SITUATIONS

  • Change of mind after processing has begun.
  • Delays caused by Government Portals (ITD/GSTN).
  • Errors due to inaccurate or late documents provided by the user.

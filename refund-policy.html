At TaxEasePro, we strive to provide high-quality, accurate, and timely tax compliance services. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please read this policy carefully. By booking or paying for any Service, you agree to these terms.

1. General Refund Rule

No Automatic Refunds After Service Commences: Once you have opted for a Service, made payment, and our team has started processing (e.g., reviewing your uploaded documents, preparing returns, or initiating filing), the fee is non-refundable.

This is because our team (Chartered Accountants, advocates, and accountants) invests time, expertise, and resources immediately upon booking to deliver the Service efficiently (often within 24 hours).

2. Exceptions – When Refunds May Be Issued

We understand mistakes can happen. If you believe your case qualifies for a refund, contact us immediately. We review requests on a case-by-case basis and issue refunds only when we determine the issue is genuine.

Possible scenarios where we may approve a full or partial refund:

  • Wrong Service Selected by Mistake: If you accidentally booked the wrong Service (e.g., ITR-3 instead of ITR-1) and inform us before we start processing (i.e., before any document review or preparation begins).
  • Technical Error on Our Side: If a clear error occurs due to our website or system (e.g., double charging).
  • Service Not Delivered Due to Our Fault: If we are unable to deliver the promised Service for reasons entirely under our control (rare).
  • Other Genuine Cases: Any situation you believe is fair and reasonable (e.g., immediate cancellation due to personal emergency).

Note: Refunds are not guaranteed and are at our sole discretion. We aim to be fair and customer-friendly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much time does ITR filing take?

For salaried individuals and freelancers, we file ITR within 24-48 hours. For complex business ITRs, it usually takes 2 to 5 working days depending on technical requirements.

What is the timeline for New GST Registration?

Typically, it takes around 7 working days to receive your GST certificate once all required documents are submitted and verified by our experts.

Is my data safe with TaxEasePro?

Yes, we use bank-grade encryption. Your financial documents and personal data are kept 100% confidential and are used strictly for your tax compliance only.

How do you ensure I get the maximum tax refund?

Our experts perform a manual audit of your profile to identify every eligible deduction (80C, 80D, HRA, etc.), ensuring you get the maximum legal refund possible.

What is the process for GST registration?

Once you contact us, we assign an expert to understand your needs. After you are satisfied, we provide a checklist of documents. Post-submission, we handle the entire process until you get the certificate.

How do you support GST monthly/quarterly returns?

Our clients stay in continuous touch with their assigned experts via WhatsApp or Call. We proactively collect data and ensure timely filing to avoid any late fees or penalties.

What if I receive a tax notice after filing?

TaxEasePro provides full support for notices. Our experts draft professional legal replies and represent your case to ensure a smooth resolution.

Do you provide Projected Balance Sheets for bank loans?

Yes, we specialize in creating professional Projected and Provisional Balance Sheets that are specifically designed to meet bank requirements for MSME and business loans.

Can I contact my assigned tax expert directly?

Yes, every client is assigned a dedicated expert. You will have their direct contact details for seamless communication throughout the process.

Do you handle GST Revocation (Cancelled GST)?

Yes, we help businesses restart their operations by handling the legal revocation process for GST registrations that have been cancelled.

What documents are needed for my ITR?

Usually, PAN, Aadhaar, Bank Statements, and Form 16 are enough. However, your assigned expert will give you a customized list based on your specific income sources.

Can I file ITR for previous years (ITR-U)?

Yes, we can help you file "Updated Returns" for previous years as per the latest government rules to keep your record clean.

Do you provide services for NRIs?

Yes, we offer specialized taxation services for NRIs, including management of NRE/NRO accounts and capital gains on property sales.

No. We believe in 100% transparency. The price we quote after the initial assessment is exactly what you pay—no hidden fees, ever.
